The rock on which we stand

The time line of Abraham Lincoln:

  • 1809 - Born in Spring Farm, Kentucky.
  • 1818 - His mother, Nancy Lincoln, passed away.
  • 1831 - He moved to Illnois.
  • 1842 He married to Mary Todd.
  • 1846 - He won the election to the U.S. House of Representatives, representing Illnois 7th district.
  • 1858 - Lincoln ran for the U.S. Senate and lost to Stephen A. Douglas. While running for the U.S. Senate, Lincoln had been advocating anti-slavery movement across the United States
  • 1860 - Lincoln won the presidency on November 6th, becoming the first Republican president to do so.
  • 1861-1865 - The civil war took place during his presidency. The southern states feared that Lincoln would abolish slavery, decided to wage war against the union. It started on April 12 1861 and ended on May 26 1865.The union prevailed over the southern states marking the end of slavery. In addition, Lincoln won the reelection in 1864.
  • 1865 - Dies at the age of 56. Lincoln was assasinated by a confederate spy.
